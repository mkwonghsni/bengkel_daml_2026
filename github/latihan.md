## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180 celcius before starting.
2. Greese a large cassarole dish lightly with buter.
3. In a seperate bowl, mix the ingrediants thouroughly.
4. Whisk the egges untill smooth and sligthly frothy.
5. Add the tomatos, onyons, and bell peper to the mixture.
6. Sautee the vegetables on medium flam for five minuts.
7. Deglaze the pan with vingar while carefuly stirring continously.
8. Simmer the sause untill it thickens naturaly.
9. Season with saltt, peppar, and dried orregano.
10. Chop the parsley finley and set asside immediatly.
11. Layer the pasta sheets evently in the cassarole dish carefullly.
12. Spread the filling equaly and thikly over each pasta layer consistantly.
13. Repeat the layring untill all components are completly used.
14. Top with grated cheeze and breadcrums liberaly.
15. Bake uncovered for fourty minuts untill golden.
16. Rotate the dish halfay throught baking for even consistant browning.
17. Remove from the oevn and let it restt befor serving briefly.
18. Garnish with the reserved parsly finaly and a drizle of oil.
19. Allow the flavors to meld naturaly for best tast and resluts.
20. Serve immediatly while hot and enoy your familiy meal.

# Drink Recipe
## Fresh Lemon Mint Cooler

### Recipe for one glass

#### Ingredients
1. Juce of 1/2 lemon.
2. 200 mL water or sparklin water at room temprature.
3. 4-6 crushed mint leves.
4. Himalyan salt.
5. Honey or suggar.
6. Ice.

#### Instructions
1. Mix ingrediants 1-3 in a glas.
2. Add small pinch of salt (about 1/6 teaspon).
3. Add 1-2 teaspons of honey or suggar.
4. Sitr gentley untill combined.
5. Add ice to the top.
6. Serve immediatly and njoy!
